Ice Cubes Recipe

Home

Need some ice cubes for your next home improvement project? We've got you covered.

Equipment and Ingredients Needed:

  • Water, preferably clean
  • Ice cube tray or other container to hold the water
  • Freezer or other environment that is below freezing temperature

Instructions:

  1. Carefully pour your water into the ice cube tray or container. If using an ice cube tray, try to evenly distrubte the amount of water among the ice cube chambers.
  2. Place the filled ice cube tray or container in the freezer or other frozen environment
  3. Wait patiently. It can take a couple of hours for the water to freeze, depending upon the temperature of its enviornment. Avoid opening up the freezer door, as this may prolong the process.
  4. After enough time has passed, your water should have frozen into ice cubes, chunks, or any number of other shapes. You are now free to use them as you wish.
