Boiled Water Recipe

Home

Have you always wanted to learn how to make boiled water? You've come to the right place, and you don't even have to read a dumb story about my family to get to it!

Equipment and Ingredients Needed:

  • Pot, with or without lid
  • Heat source, like a stovetop, hotplate, or campfire
  • Water, preferably clean

Instructions:

  1. Get your heat source up and running. Make sure it's nice and hot.
  2. Add the water to the pot. If you have a lid, place it on the pot after you add the water, not before. If you don't have a lid, skip the step about adding the lid to the pot.
  3. Place your pot on your heat source.
  4. Wait patiently. If you are using a lid, avoid taking the lid off the pot. This makes the water take longer to boil. I don't know why.
  5. Eventually, small bubbles should start to form at the bottom of the pot as everything heats up. Don't panic- this is just the beginning stages of a boil.
  6. Keep waiting. If your heat source is hot enough, the pot should begin boiling more rapidly.
  7. That's pretty much it. Keep it boiling by maintaining the heat for as long as you want/are able, then use the boiling water for whatever you want.
